To Bridget

發表於 2017-11-26 | 分類於 Private

Dear Bridget, Mr. and Mrs. O’Rourke,

Thank you so much for your hospitable receptions during the Thanksgiving! It was my first time to experience American family life and I felt as if I’m at home. Thank you Mr. and Mrs. O’Rourke for all the lovely food on Thanksgiving night and the nice environment you prepared. Doylestown is such a livable and impressive place with the attractive countryside view and starry night which I can hardly see in the city.

I hope to visit Doylestown and your house again in the future, and my family and I also sincerely welcome you to come visit China one day. Finally, Happy Thanksgiving to you!

Best,
Shaoming (Eureka) Zheng 鄭少銘

To Annika
