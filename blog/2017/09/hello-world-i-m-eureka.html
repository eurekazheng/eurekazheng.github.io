Hello World! I’m Shaoming (Eureka) Zheng.

Facts about me

醉後不知天在水，滿船清夢壓星河。

餘廣東潮州府人氏。自幼甚喜讀書，及弱冠，赴美利堅國留學，攻讀計算機工程，已半載矣。作此手記，以抒時下興懷。餘閒暇時好撫琴，鋼琴結它皆能奏也；又好攝影之術。書則紅樓爲獨愛。

My Chinese name is 鄭少銘(Zhèng Shào Míng), or 鄭少銘 in Traditional characters. I come from Swatow, Guangdong, Southern China. I can speak English, Mandarin, Cantonese, and Teochew dialect.

I’m a Computer Engineering freshman in University of Pittsburgh.

I do well in Programming, Robotics, Chinese literature, Music (Piano & Guitar), and Photography.

I love Cantonese cuisine (Dim sum), Philosophy, and Impressionism Art.

I use tab to indent. I do not start the bracket in a new line. I use Emacs.

My favorite book is Dream of the Red Chamber(紅樓夢) or The Story of the Stone(石頭記). I studied Redology (a subject studying this book)

I’m Agnostic and Skeptical.
